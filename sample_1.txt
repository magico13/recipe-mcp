Beef & Broccoli Bowl (Wegmans)

Quick Dinners
Prep Time: 5 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins | Servings: Serving size is 1 bowl

Ingredients:

1 tsp olive oil
1 lb 80/20 ground beef
1/2 tbsp soy sauce
1/2 cup hoisin sauce
1 tsp toasted sesame oil
20 oz Organic Frozen Jasmine Rice Steamables
12 oz frozen broccoli stems & florets

Directions:

Step 1: Heat oil in stir-fry pan on HIGH.
Step 2: Add beef; cook 2-3 min to brown. Cook, stirring to break up pieces, 1-2 min until beef is about 75% cooked through.
Step 3: Remove beef from pan; drain fat from pan.
Step 4: Add beef back to pan; continue to cook on HIGH. Stir in soy sauce and hoisin. Cook, stirring, about 1 min until sauces have glazed beef and beef is cooked through.
Step 5: Remove pan from heat. Add sesame oil, stir to incorporate.
Step 6: Cook rice and broccoli per package directions. Add broccoli to pan with beef; stir to combine.
Step 7: Divide rice and beef-broccoli mixture evenly into four bowls.
